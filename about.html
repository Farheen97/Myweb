ABOUT US

About the Experience :

Fun World is one of Bangalore’s oldest entertainment hubs built to entertain thrill-seeking enthusiasts of all ages. It homes 50 rides across four categories: kids, adults, family and adrenaline. Try the Fun Express that takes everyone for a spin around the park or Hang Glider to sway on a 50-feet high flight - both family rides. For adrenaline junkies the Crazy Fireball and Frisbee rides in the amusement section and Devil’s Drop and Boomerang in the water section are must-dos.

Park timings

Fun World: 11:00 A.M. - 7:00 P.M.
Water World: 11:00 A.M. - 4:30 P.M.

Redemption process

After the booking is complete, you will receive an email with a booking confirmation voucher that can also be viewed on the Cleartrip app in the ‘bookings’ section of your profile Present the trip ID mentioned in the voucher at the redemption counter to collect physical tickets
Show tickets to park authorities to enter
Know before you go
Rides will operate subject to availability
One or more rides may be non-operational due to shutdown maintenance
A valid Indian photo ID is mandatory to enter the park
Children aged three years and below need to buy physical tickets at the counter
Children must be accompanied by parents or guardians at all times
Appropriate swimwear (preferably nylon) must be worn at all times when using the park’s water attractions
Outside food and drinks are not allowed inside the park
Smoking within the park premises is not permitted
